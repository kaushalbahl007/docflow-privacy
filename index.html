Privacy Policy

DocSnap — PDF Scanner & Editor

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DocSnap ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. DocSnap is a PDF scanner and editing tool that is designed to work offline and on your device. Please read this policy carefully. By using DocSnap, you agree to the practices described here.

The short version: Your documents never leave your phone. DocSnap processes every scan, edit, merge, sign, and conversion locally on your device. We do not upload, store, sell, or share your files or their contents on any server.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

DocSnap does not collect, transmit, or store:

  • The contents of your PDFs, scans, or images.
  • Text extracted through OCR or search.
  • Your signatures, annotations, passwords, or document metadata.
  • Your name, email, phone number, or contacts.
  • Your precise location.

All documents you create or open, and any data derived from them, remain in the app's private storage on your device. If you uninstall the app, that data is removed with it.

2. Information Processed On Your Device

To provide its features, the app accesses certain data locally, without sending it anywhere:

FeatureWhat it accesses (on-device only)
Document scanningYour camera, to capture pages you choose to scan.
Import / Image → PDFPhotos and files you explicitly select from your device.
Document VaultYour device biometric / screen-lock, only to unlock the vault. We never receive your fingerprint or PIN.
RemindersLocal notifications for document expiry dates you set. These are scheduled on the device.
Search & OCRText recognition runs on-device using Google ML Kit's on-device models.

3. Permissions We Request

Android may ask you to grant the following permissions. Each is used only for the stated purpose, and most are optional depending on which features you use:

  • Camera — to scan documents. Used only while you are scanning.
  • Photos / Storage — to let you import images or PDFs you choose, and to save your output files.
  • Biometric — to unlock the optional Document Vault using your device's fingerprint / face / screen lock.
  • Notifications — to show local reminders for document expiry dates you set.
  • Internet / Network state — required by the underlying app platform and for optional purchases (see below). The app does not upload your documents.

4. Purchases

DocSnap may offer an optional paid upgrade ("DocSnap Pro"). Purchases are handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not see or store your payment card details. Google processes the transaction and shares only the purchase status needed to unlock premium features. Google's handling of your payment information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

DocSnap relies on the following third-party components:

  • Google ML Kit (on-device) — powers text recognition (OCR) and document detection. Processing happens on your device; document text is not sent to Google for these features.
  • Google Play Services / Play Billing — for app distribution and optional in-app purchases, as described above.

If a future version of the app introduces any service that transmits data off your device, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for your consent first.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or your documents with third parties. The only time a document leaves the app is when you explicitly choose to share it — for example, using the Share button to send a file to another app (WhatsApp, email, cloud storage, etc.). At that point the receiving app's own privacy policy applies.

7. Data Security

Your files are stored in the app's private, sandboxed storage that other apps cannot access. The optional Document Vault adds a biometric / screen-lock layer. For password-protected PDFs, encryption is applied on your device using industry- standard AES. Because no document data is transmitted to us, there is no server-side copy of your files to be breached.

8. Children's Privacy

DocSnap is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights & Control

Because your data lives on your device, you are always in control. You can:

  • Delete any document from within the app at any time.
  • Revoke Camera, Photos, or Notification permissions in your device settings.
  • Clear the app's cache from the app's Settings screen.
  • Remove all app data by uninstalling DocSnap.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be reflected in the app or its store listing.
